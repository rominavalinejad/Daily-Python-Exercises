## Challenge: Daily Fruit Inventory Log

1. Create a dictionary named fruit_price with the following initial items:
   {"Apple": 1500, "Bannana": 1000, "Orage": 1200}.
3. Print the initial dictionary and the total number of items using the len() function.
4. Update the price of "Bannana" to 1100 and completely remove "Apple" from the dictionary.
5. Print the updated dictionary and the new item count.
6. Add a new fruit "Mango" with a price of 2000, adjust the "Bannana" price to 1400, and re-introduce "Apple" with a price of 1500.
7. Print the final inventory dictionary and its total item count.
